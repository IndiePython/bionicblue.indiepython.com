General info

Warning! This game is at an early stage of development. At the moment it works like a demo to showcase action/animations.

Blue is a bionic boy fighting to protect humanity against hordes of dangerous robots programmed to bring the extinction of humanity. Help him discover what's causing the robot rampage while he looks for his missing friends and try to put an end to this menace.

Genre Action, platformer
Developer Kennedy R. S. Guerra
Price $0 (free), public domain; consider donating
Status in development
Release date[1] first half of 2023
Made with pygame
Platform(s) Windows, Mac and GNU/Linux
Mode(s) single-player
Language(s) English
How many levels 05 levels with bosses (includes intro and final level)
